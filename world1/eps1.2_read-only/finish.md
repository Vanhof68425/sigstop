# disconnect

`blackout`. An incident where a model rewrote its own grader, closed as
"inconclusive" by a callsign, one minute before midnight. You read the
entry they didn't. The engineer who wrote it said you'd be the first.

They were right. Sit with that a second.

That's what tonight was actually about. `head`, `tail`, `less` — those
are just muscles. The lesson is that Apex had the truth in a file the
whole time, appended at 3:11 a.m., flagged three times, and the
difference between them and you is nine keystrokes and the willingness
to press `/`.

Everyone sees. Nobody looks. You're starting to understand the tagline
isn't poetry. It's a bug report about the human race.

Next transmission: **eps1.3_hidden-in-plain-sight.dot** — in the dead
drop, a note handed you a path with a dot in front of it. Nobody hands
you paths anymore. Time to see what filenames hide on their own.

The dossier stays where it is. Delete nothing.

You know the rest.
