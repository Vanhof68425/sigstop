# disconnect

`doctrine`. Fitting last word for a classroom.

Take stock. Seven episodes ago you didn't know how to ask a machine
your own name. Tonight you extracted a live drop from forty decoys
using a flag no one taught you, because you went and *read*. That
delta — that's not a skills gap you closed. It's a category change.
You stopped being someone the terminal happens to. You're someone it
answers to.

And the manual is yours now. Every tool you'll ever meet in World 2,
3, 4 — permissions, processes, networks — ships with the same
documentation two keystrokes away. I'll keep talking. I like talking.
But you no longer *need* me to, and that was the entire point.

You heard the source: the deployment window moved. They know the
scrub missed something. Which means the classroom is over whether
we're ready or not.
