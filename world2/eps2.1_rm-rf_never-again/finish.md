found the capture, my first thought was *never again* — never another

accidents. Accidents don't take promises.

your life. Thousands of times, tired, rushed, sure of yourself. The
promise isn't that you'll never face the twelfth job. It's that you'll
`ls` first on every single one of the eleven before it, so that when
the twelfth comes, looking isn't a decision anymore.

It's just what your hands do.
